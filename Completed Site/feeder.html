Birds of Tokyo

Birds of Tokyo originated from Perth Western Australia in 2004, this five-piece alternative rock band gained initial success with their debut album Day One. Day One reached no. 3 on the AIR Indeoendant Album Charts and stayed in the top 10 for 36 consecutive weeks.

The Wombats

The Wombats are compromised of Matthew Murphy (lead vocals, guitar, keyboard), Daniel Haggis (drums, percussio, keyboard, backing vocals) and Tord Overland Knudsen (bass guitar, guitar, keyboard, backing vocals). They are an English indie rock band that was formed in liverpool 2003.
